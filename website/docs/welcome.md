# Welcome

Welcome to the Orcada Stake Pool guides. These guides aim to help you build and operate a Cardano Stake Pool that is 
resilient, has low energy consumption, utilising ARM based infrastructure.

The focus of the guides is on the specific infrastructure architecture and design decisions of our pool. Namely using 
ARM devices (for example Honeycomb LX2 and Raspberry Pi), dual network and ISP, and NAS for PXE boot.

These guides were initially created as our team's internal operational resource and are specific to our infrastructure 
setup. We have decided to make the guides publicly available to help other Stake Pool operators who may benefit from 
the lessons we have learned.

We are an Australian based Stake Pool, and are proud members of the Armada Alliance, Bare Metal Alliance and Cardano 
Single Pool Alliance communities. We highly recommend these Cardano communities which embody and promote the Cardano 
principles of infrastructure decentralisation. These communities also offer a wide range of resources focusing on other 
architectures, devices and design choices.
